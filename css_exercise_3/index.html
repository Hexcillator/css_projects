Kale Caeser Salad with Roasted Chicken

Kale and Quinoa provide a healthy base for roasted chicken topped with a light Caesar sauce.

Total time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/4 Cup Kale
  • 1 Cup Quinoa
  • 2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Chicken Breast
  • Caesar Dressing

Preparation

  1. Prepare quinoa and roast chicken until brown and 165 in middle.

    Time: 45 minutes

  2. Toss quinoa, chicken, kale and Caesar dressing until coated

    Time: 4 minutes

  3. Add walnuts and olive oil as garnish

    Time: 1 minute
